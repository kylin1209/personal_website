About Me

My name is Kylie Lin and I am currently an undergraduate student at New York University. I am pursuing my Computer Science degree with a minor in Mathematics and will be graduating in December 2026

As a sophomore undergraduate, I'm passionate about the transformative potential of data visualization and machine learning. I believe that turning raw data into visual insights can drive impactful decision-making and deepen our understanding of complex patterns. I’m particularly interested in the intersection of these fields, where I can apply machine learning techniques to uncover hidden trends and make data accessible and compelling. Through my coursework and projects, I’m developing the technical skills needed to create meaningful visualizations and optimize algorithms, and I’m excited to continue exploring these tools to bridge data science with real-world applications.

I have had previous internship experience right out of high school and after my freshman year at Rutgers University. I am particularly familiar with DevOps methodologies such as Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD), Agile, and the Software Development Life Cycle, as well as AWS and Jira Platforms.

In June 2023, I graduated from High Technology High School in Lincroft, New Jersey. During my time as a student there, I was the President of the Class of 2023, our National Honors Society's Vice President, and an awarded researcher by the Delaware Valley Science Fair. High Technology High School was consistently ranked as the #1 STEM public school during my time there and still is today.

I am currently open to research and/or internship roles revolving around software enginerring, data visualization, modeling, imaging, and machine learning. My experiences, projects, and awards are all listed on the Experiences page!

Work

Software Engineering Intern at Bessemer Trust (Summer 2025)

DevOps Intern at Maximus (May 2024 - August 2024; January 2025 - May 2025): During my summer at Maximus, I was immersed in the world of automation and DevOps. I developed Python scripts to streamline ticketing, enabling the IT department to respond more efficiently to requests. Additionally, I created a course on Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) tailored for the DevOps Digital Solutions team, which facilitated smoother workflows and enhanced the team's certification process. My experience also involved troubleshooting in JIRA, where I resolved technical issues, ensuring continuous team productivity and project momentum.

Automation Project Specialist at TetherView (February 2023 - April 2024): At TetherView, I focused on leveraging automation to support marketing and business growth. By integrating various government APIs, I helped the marketing team reach new customer segments, such as companies filing IPOs. My role required thorough research and proactive program development to identify potential business leads, enhancing the company’s market intelligence. This experience underscored my understanding of API integration and gave me a firsthand look at how automation can drive sales and marketing strategies.

Technology Lead at CliMit (September 2023 - December 2023): As Technology Lead for CliMit, a climate-change project, I directed the technical aspects for a small team working on a conceptual app to address environmental challenges. My role involved designing the application structure and providing technical guidance, alongside my responsibilities as a developer. Presenting our project, I highlighted how tech could empower individuals to engage with climate action. Our innovative concept earned us the IDEA Award, recognizing our approach and encouraging me to continue pursuing impactful tech solutions.
